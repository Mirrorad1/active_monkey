## Wall 1 — the growth valley (Exp 143–146; parked)

A unimodal emission model of a multimodal world cannot be grown by LOCAL moves
(add a component, split a component) under ANY honest short-horizon acceptance
test, replayed or lived: under log-loss, an incomplete tight cover loses to a
complete loose cover, because misses cost more nats than hits gain. Three designs
failed from three sides (dishonest replay vote; honest test correctly rejecting
add; honest test correctly rejecting split — probation surges +0.6…+3.4 nats in
all three). What works inside the wall: the surprise-ceiling detector (precise,
8/8), the live-probation acceptance test (70–86% sustained benefit among keeps),
and covariance self-regulation (learned Σ → 0.90–0.98× the true within-color
scatter — aliased senses are automatically down-weighted). **Named untried
cracks:** batch-jump (fit the complete cover at once, probate it as one move);
background-floor (a permanent broad component makes additions provably hole-free).

**AMENDED at Exp 152–154 (the cracks were run on the human's word):** both cracks
fail under the standard convention — and the Exp 152 autopsy + Exp 153 diagnostic +
Exp 154 fresh-seed confirmation re-bound the wall: it belongs to the
UNNORMALIZED-FOOTPRINT EVALUATION convention (footprints capped at 1, so sharpness
buys no loudness and any K-fold refinement divides its voice by K), not to online
growth. Under normalized predictive evaluation (the conjugate update untouched),
batch-jump growth answers the alarm completely: 100% acceptance, zero detector
events, every color quiet, 24/24 fresh-seed runs (verifier-checked). The
normalized-predictive switch is a standing consult for the creature.

## Wall 2 — the valence range limit (Exp 147–149; accepted)

Predictability-grounded valence on the continuous substrate preserves ORDERING
(Spearman > 0.6 in 23/24 runs) but compresses DYNAMIC RANGE (~0.65 vs the twin's
~0.84 reliable-half share). Two mechanism hypotheses (miscalibrated bar; port
infidelity) were each predeclared, tested, and refuted; the residue is the
substrate's own: learned Gaussian footprints retain neighbor-overlap entropy that
categorical columns shed, so the continuous creature can never be as CERTAIN as a
converged table — and certainty is what this valence is made of. For the moonshot,
ordering is the load-bearing property, and ordering is intact.
