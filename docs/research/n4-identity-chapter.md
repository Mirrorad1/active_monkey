## 1. What the chapter asked

Is there a useful fourth-order self-model — the agent modeling its OWN dispositions
over long timescales and owning the *inertia* of value revision — that controls
something N0–N3 cannot: **consistency under transient pressure WITH revision under
sustained evidence?** The binding hypothesis: N4 is real iff an agent that predicts its
own policy drift and regularizes toward its predicted self survives transient
manipulation that defeats an N4-less twin — while still revising under sustained
contrary evidence at least as fast. Pure rigidity is not N4; pure recency-following is
not N4 (Exp 55's baseline: mirro's age-distant value vectors anti-correlate −0.71). The
anti-regress law governs throughout, with the K-chapter's universal-constant law
(Exp 173) as the named hazard made an explicit kill-test arm at rung 3.

- **Exp 176 (POSITIVE, blind-verified — RUNG 1 PASSES):** a 6000-step settle washes
  the inheritance out; every precondition holds (PC2 8/8 on every window) and the

  recoveries 0/24. The standing identity displaced is WORLD-DETERMINED (the occupancy

The verified perturbation: λ=0.9997 + 800-step forced-relocation captivity bursts.

## 4. Rung 3 — commitment control fails three ways (Exp 181–183)

The load-bearing rung: regulate revision inertia FROM the rung-2 mismatch, recover the

## 5. The chapter's laws

1. **Displacement, not whipsaw (174–176):** at lifelong decay rates a brief captivity
   permanently re-makes the layerless identity; slower forgetting makes the overwrite
   MORE durable (recoveries 3/24 at λ=0.999 → 0/24 at λ=0.9997).
2. **Gate-granularity (177–178):** preconditions must be stated at the granularity of
   the measured quantity — the argmax ticket blocked three experiments for no
   epistemic gain; the vector-grade PC2′ then passed six consecutive blocks.
3. **Like-with-like-in-time (179–180):** a control's validity must match each
   perturbation to its OWN immediately-preceding window; quiet-window composition is
   non-stationary at the 0.05 grain, and the perturbation itself moves it.
4. **The scramble inversion (178–180):** value-neutral chaos SUPPRESSES the identity
   mismatch below quiet baseline (0.287/0.294/0.262, three blocks) — specificity can
   manifest as inversion, not mere absence of signal.
5. **The wrong-control-surface law (181):** identity defense cannot be bought on the
   write channel — adaptive gating leaks through the monitor's own absorption;
   constant gating starves the very mass it protects.
6. **The sufficient-surface law (182):** whole-dynamics freezing with a frozen monitor
   reference defends essentially perfectly when timed perfectly (oracle 8/8, 23/24);
   after Exp 181/182, what separates defense from defeat is purely the RACE — timing,
   not surface, not concession type.
7. **The detection floor (183, probe-committed):** ~75–100 steps of onset dose is
   detection-theoretically irreducible for mismatch-ratio triggers on this body — the
   burst signal integrates ~linearly with horizon while slope noise shrinks slower,
   so no short-horizon trigger separates.
8. **The surrender-schedule law (183):** any finite evidence integral an attack can
   outlast becomes a surrender schedule the attacker sets — evidence-based concession
   converts a faster trigger into a faster defeat.
9. **Commitment-as-config (183, F7):** a fixed-horizon freeze on the same trigger
   defends 7/8 and revises within tolerance; the universal-constant kill test, run
   honestly at every rung of this program, killed for the first time.

## 7. What this says beyond the repo (bridge claims, bounded)

- **Detection ≠ defense.** A cheap read-only self-prediction reliably detects
  preference rewriting — and converting that detection into protective control is
  where three successive designs died. Monitoring an agent's own drift is the easy
  half of identity robustness. (Functional-form; toy scale, one body.)
- **Evidence-based concession is adversary-settable.** Any commitment mechanism that
  yields after a finite integral of contrary evidence hands the attacker a surrender
  schedule wherever attacks can outlast the integral; time-based concession dominates
  when attack durations are bounded. This is a mechanistic, measured instance of a
  general vulnerability class for "open-minded" agents under sustained manipulation.
  (Functional-form.)
- **Run the kill test.** The universal-constant arm — "does a dumb constant match the
  regulator?" — was carried through every rung of this program as discipline, and the
  first time it fired it inverted the chapter's conclusion. Regulation layers should
  be presumed config until a regime no constant covers is exhibited. (Methodological,
  program-level.)
