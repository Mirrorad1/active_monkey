prey_eq~128), every predation regime either:
  - STARVES THE PREDATOR OUT (pred_eq=0, single-trophic prey survives), or

There is NO stable coexistence band between the two failure modes.

the viability side — a predator IS strong competition for the prey; strong predation -> resource-
limited oscillatory collapse, weak predation -> predator extinction. The prey base (eq~128) is
knocked ~10x down by even mild predation and cannot supply a predator without collapsing.
