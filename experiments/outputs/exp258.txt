VERDICT: POSITIVE-PARTIAL — coexistence-posing is topology/scale-robust (REFUGE_ONLY + BOTH =
10/10 across {ring, grid2d, small-world} x {8, 16, 32}; NEITHER = 0/10 everywhere). The
migration+asynchrony rescue is lattice-favored and weakens at large N. Rung 3 substantially
passes; rung 4 (Red Queen trait-invasion) is UN-BLOCKED on the robust refuge/BOTH regime.

IMPLICATION: Discrete predator-prey COEXISTENCE-POSING is ROBUST (not a ring-8 knife-edge): the
refuge mechanism poses bounded global coexistence across every tested topology and scale. The two
rescue mechanisms differ in generality: REFUGE is topology/scale-general; MIGRATION+ASYNCHRONY is
a lattice-locality effect (ring/grid) that erodes when long-range edges (small-world) or large
patch counts blur the local-asynchrony structure. A robustly-coexisting resident exists
everywhere (BOTH/refuge regime, 10/10) -> Red Queen trait-invasion rung is UN-BLOCKED on that
regime.
