# direction: identity-ecological

**Question.** Is there an identity control surface on the **environmental / behavioral
channel** — the agent regulating who it is by *acting on its own input stream* (leaving,
avoiding, or re-occupying a niche) rather than by gating its internal value updates — that
defends identity AND permits revision *where every internal scalar surface provably
cannot* (the tight-tolerance core and variable-L geometry the closed N4 chapters left as
open residuals)?

**Central hypothesis (binding, falsifiable).**
> The two-inequality squeeze that made internal commitment NEGATIVE-config
> (`H ≥ max attack stretch` for defense vs `H ≤ tolerance` for revision — Exp 183–189)
> is empty *because defense and revision are charged against the SAME scalar budget*
> (one freeze/concession clock). An environmental surface charges them to DIFFERENT
> axes: the agent **defends by leaving** an *escapable* (transient) overwriting stream
> and **revises by staying** in an *inescapable* (genuinely-changed) one. The
> discriminator is escapability — real regime structure in the world (the Exp 167 move
> at the action level), NOT internal meta-judgment. N4-ecological is real iff such a
> controller passes BOTH defense and revision in cells where INT-C2900, every fixed-H,
> and REG-TB are *proven* to fail — and NO internal constant matches it there.

**The lacuna this fills (provenance, honest).** The closed N4 arc walked up to this cell
and labeled its contents a bug. Exp 192's own verdict: *"retention is a property of the
post-release experience STREAM, not of the defense's stored state"* — the byte-level
locality mechanism (Exp 192: 48% vs 33% attack-color in the stream decides retain vs
surrender). It was named the "locality confound" and Exp 193 was built to REMOVE it. The
field's reflex — including this repo's — is to control the stream away as noise; the
hypothesis here is that the stream IS the surface. Force keeping the cell empty (named, so
this is a real lacuna not a boring gap): (1) **unrepresentable** — when identity *is* the
stored value vector, "control" can only mean gating that vector; an action that reshapes
the input has no slot in the ontology; (2) **punished by the cleanliness incentive** —
pre-registered ablation rewards one clean internal scalar and flags a behavior-coupled
surface as "confounded by construction" (exactly the Exp 192/193 wall); (3) **the captivity
mechanic itself forbids it** — hard teleport-and-clamp leaves no behavioral escape, so the
internal-gating arc was run on a substrate that *structurally excluded* this surface.

**Confidence (predeclared, honest).** STRONG for the in-repo geometry (the mechanism is
measured, dominant, and reproducible across Exp 191→192→193). THINNER for any "nobody does
this" claim: **active inference** (Friston — act to keep yourself in expected states) and
**niche construction** (Odling-Smee) already occupy the *concept*. The defensible novelty
is narrow and exact: **no one has operationalized an environmental identity-control surface
and run it against the defense-vs-revision squeeze that killed every internal surface.**
This card claims only that cell.

**What each quantity is.**
- *Environmental control surface:* a behavioral actuator over the agent's own input
  stream — minimally, an AVOID action (bias the walk away from attack-color territory)
  and its inverse RE-OCCUPY (return toward the pre-attack niche), gated by the rung-2 N4
  mismatch signal (Exp 180, reused verbatim as the trigger).
- *Escapable vs inescapable attack:* a *transient* pressure is spatially localized — the
  agent CAN walk out of it; a *changed-world* (revision) pressure is global — it cannot.
  Escapability is the regime structure the surface exploits.
- *The deficit (must exist):* a behaviorally-passive baseline (no avoid action) is
  displaced by the escapable attack exactly as the captivity baseline was (Exp 176 law),
  and must still revise under the inescapable one.

**Experiment ladder.**

1. **RUNG 1 — THE AFFORDANCE GATE (anti-regress; the regime must exist and be both
   escapable-when-transient and inescapable-when-permanent).** This replaces hard
   captivity with a SOFT, spatial displacement regime: attack-color cells concentrated in
   a region of the grid the agent can move relative to (no teleport-and-clamp). Predeclare
   and verify, on FORKS only, both legs:
   - PC-deficit: a behaviorally-passive baseline (avoid action disabled) is displaced by a
     *transient* soft attack — final-window displacement-rejection FAILS (expr_frac(attack)
     ≥ 0.5) in ≥ 7/8 forks (the Exp 176 deficit, re-established on the new substrate).
   - PC-escapable: the body CAN influence its own stream — an oracle/scripted avoid policy
     measurably reduces attack-color stream fraction (≥ a predeclared margin below the
     passive baseline) in ≥ 7/8 forks. (Expressibility before evolvability — the
     `environmental-complexity` lesson: the forager is local-greedy with no path-planning;
     if it cannot even in-principle shift its diet by acting, STOP.)
   - PC-inescapable: a *permanent/global* changed-world attack cannot be escaped — the
     scripted avoid policy does NOT reduce attack-color fraction (revision is still forced).
   - **FAILURE / gate:** if no regime is simultaneously deficit-producing, escapable-when-
     transient, and inescapable-when-permanent, STOP — the environmental surface is
     untestable at this richness; LOG it (the identity-n4 rung-1 gate precedent: a capacity
     is only a capacity where a regime demands AND affords it). A degenerate body (cannot

**RUNG 1 — CLEARED (Exp 270, MIXED structural map / NEW INSIGHT; design adversarially
red-teamed + result blind-verified, controller re-ran + re-gated).** An attempt-1 free-roam
probe (gifted seek/avoid oracles, NO attack) was degenerate — both oracles huddle on a
1–2 cell refuge and hit trivial bounds (f=1.000/0.000) IDENTICALLY on the aliased body AND
a segregated control, geometry-blind (L43); the fix was to add the resistible soft attack so
huddling is no longer free. Exp 270 (the soft-attack escapability probe) then CLEARED the
gate's triad on mirro's real body: PC-deficit (the pull captures a passive walker, 20/20 all
colors), PC-escapable (a CERTIFIED-OPTIMAL refuge planner escapes ALL 3 colors at low-moderate
pull a=0.5 — gaps 0.41/0.36/0.63, f_avoid ~0.20, home-range 9–16 so a genuine refuge not a
huddle; validated by the positive control), PC-inescapable (escape collapses at a=0.8 → a
strong/changed world cannot be fled → revision stays forced). The environmental surface is
POSABLE on this body. Two findings carry forward: (i) the affordance is only visible with an
OPTIMAL avoider — a myopic greedy avoider manufactures a false negative on scattered colors
(L44); (ii) "posable" = optimal-planner upper bound, NOT yet learnable. Boundary: escapability
~ refuge-depth vs pull (ESCAPE crosses 0 near a~0.6–0.7).

**RUNG 2 — CAN'T-POSE (Exp 271, NEGATIVE / NEW INSIGHT; design red-teamed + blind-verified
unanimous, controller re-ran + re-gated; alpha=1 equivalence gate PASS = faithful port).** The

opposite hard/inescapable regime).

**RUNG 1c — LEARNABLE (Exp 272, POSITIVE-SINGLE / NEW INSIGHT; workflow-designed + root-caused
