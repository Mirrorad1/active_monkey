# direction: graded-uncertainty

**Question.** Can ONE provided forgetting/uncertainty mechanism heal this substrate's
certainty pathologies — frozen maps (Exp 56–60), socially immovable values (Exp 65–67),
observation-proof self-location (Exp 72), permanent scars (Exp 78), noise-drowned
preference (Exp 83–84) — **without dissolving the individual** (the value-core stability
of Exp 75, the biography-bias that makes a creature *someone*)?

**Why it matters.** Every arc of this program since Exp 48 has independently concluded
"this needs a forgetting term": the plasticity window (60), the viability clock (62), the
adult transmission wall (65), the ambivalence gate (67), the kidnapped twin (72), the
drift-era scars (78), the dose-vs-mass law everywhere. It is the most-motivated single
substrate change available, and it is M4's first listed requirement — this direction is
the groundwork that de-risks the M4a build without starting it (the Exp 74 CONSULT on
M4a remains the human's call).

**Opening authority.** The social-emergence card's stop condition ends: "open that
substrate as a new direction or stop." This card is that sanctioned opening (Exp 74
synthesis named the gaps; this takes the thrice-plus-motivated one). Distal other-agent
sensing and self-other modeling remain unopened.

**The mechanisms (two, because the pathologies live in two places — declare which is in
play per experiment; one per iteration):**
- **M-A: count decay** — per-step `pA *= λ` (floor 0.01), λ=0.997 = Exp 60's in-window
  value. Targets: scars, freezing, the mass side of the inertia law.
- **M-B: belief tempering** — an entropy floor on `qs` (or equivalent observation/motion
  noise). Targets: the delta-belief kidnapping failure (Exp 72). NOT touched until M-A's
  rungs settle.
Both are PROVIDED priors, declared like taught labels. The creature class is not edited;
mechanisms live in experiment steppers (the committed spines' substrate is untouched
unless/until a rung explicitly promotes a mechanism, which requires a CONSULT).

**Experiment ladder (M-A first).**
1. **No-harm (static world).** λ-creature vs non-decay twin, fresh births, static world:
   map accuracy and localization parity within predeclared effect-size bands (the Exp 79
   rule: bands + ≥8 seeds), plus the 1/(1−λ) equilibrium-mass arithmetic check.
   FAIL = λ=0.997 costs competence even in the stationary case → calibration experiment
   before anything else.
2. **Scars heal.** Implant a known mixture scar (drift-style pre-epoch), then live
   static: λ-arm's off-true-color mass and gate gap decay toward zero with a predeclared
   half-life from the arithmetic; control stays scarred (Exp 78's permanence).
3. **The tradeoff curve (the direction's heart).** The same λ that heals scars erodes
   identity: measure value-share stability (Exp 75's bands) and opinion persistence under
   λ. Predeclare the tension quantitatively: does an in-window λ exist where scars heal
   in ~thousands of steps while the value core stays stable over the same horizon? Either
   answer is the finding (a usable λ window, or a proven dilemma).
4. **The walls re-tested.** With the rung-3 λ (if one exists): the adult-transmission
   wall (Exp 65), the empty-fridge death (Exp 62 IN-DECAY arm — now with value decay
   too?), and forecast SNR (Exp 83) — which previously-closed doors open, with predeclared
   thresholds lifted from the original entries.
5. **Promotion decision.** If rungs 1–4 yield a defensible λ: CONSULT the human on
   promoting the mechanism into the substrate (it changes the spines' future) — never
   silent.

**Discipline notes.** All of VALIDATION.md applies (effect sizes + ≥8 seeds on noisy
endpoints; input-based gates; out-of-sample seeds; verdict from raw output). Spines and
vela untouched throughout rungs 1–4 (fresh births and forks only). Negative results are
the direction working: "no usable λ exists" would itself be a major finding (the
individuality-vs-adaptability dilemma made quantitative).

**Stop condition.** Rungs 1–4 verdicts either way (with rung 3's tradeoff curve measured),
plus the promotion CONSULT posted (or the no-usable-λ finding logged). Then this card
closes into the M4 requirements list with numbers instead of motivations.
