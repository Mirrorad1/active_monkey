# online-structure-growth-v0

**Status: validated (toy scale) — and a methodological boundary.**

Detector-triggered, creature-accepted autonomous model growth (detect inadequacy → grow latent
structure → quiet) runs end-to-end (24/24 runs, 100% acceptance, zero detector events in the
final 1000 steps across three layouts) **once predictive evaluation uses normalized mixture
densities**.

The reusable lesson is the **methodological boundary**: the prior five-design "growth wall"
(Exp 143–151) belonged to the **capped-footprint evaluation convention** (unnormalized
footprints cap at 1 regardless of sharpness, so splitting a broad component into K sharp pieces
divides predictive voice by K for nothing — the exact arithmetic, ~2.7 nats, that produced the
observed probation penalty, Exp 152 autopsy), **not** to the geometry of online growth. Switch
to normalized densities — conjugate update untouched — and growth becomes honest (Exp 154,
breakthrough).

**Honesty:** one growth mechanism (batch-jump) under one acceptance protocol; the detector
threshold (0.7) was reused on the normalized scale where it is conservative; "wall fell" is
bounded to this world family and toy scale. The M4 valence-range narrowing under normalization
is a standing **prediction**, not a confirmed result. Source: Exp 152–154.
